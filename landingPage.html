At FLWR we are firm believers in detail. We share a passion for both gorgeous
flowers and awe-striking design. This passion drives us to be utterly uncompromising
in the quality of our product. “Good enough” is never quite. From our first love of flowers,
to linens to lighting design down to every last little detail, our mission is to create
an atmosphere that will transport your guests seamlessly into a world of your making.
Gathering with the people we love and creating an atmosphere set apart from normal life through
decor is a human practice more ancient than farming. It is fundamentally human to celebrate and celebrate well!
